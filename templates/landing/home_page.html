Tracking

The main feature of rush hound is its tracking capabilities. It allows greek life groups to create new potential members, comment on them, and assign them to members of the organization as a point of contact.

Ranking

Once all of your new recruits are listed in rush hound you have the ability to allow certain members of your organization the ability to rank them. These rankings can be used to give a general portayal of members feelings towards a particular new recruit.

Events

Rush hound allows you to enter events and track which recruits show up to those events. This way you can see which new recruits are willing to commit time to join your organization.
